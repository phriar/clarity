An Intentional Investment

Designed for women at a meaningful turning point

Clarity Uncorked is intentionally small, deeply curated, and designed for women standing at a meaningful turning point.

This is not a typical retreat — and it's not meant to be. Over the course of a week in Southwest France, you'll step out of your daily roles and into a thoughtfully facilitated experience that blends coaching, conversation, cultural immersion, and spacious time to reflect.

Every detail is designed to support clarity, connection, and forward movement — before you arrive, while we're together, and after you return home.

We keep the cohort small by design. Fewer voices. Deeper conversations. Space to be fully present with yourself and others.

Clarity Uncorked is for women who are ready to pause with intention, invest in themselves without apology, and leave with a clearer sense of what comes next.

If you're feeling a quiet pull rather than a rush of certainty, that's often the right place to begin.

What's Not Included

A few things to plan for

Here's what falls outside the retreat so you can budget accordingly and travel prepared.

  • Airfare to and from Bordeaux
  • Any alcohol or meals outside of the curated agenda
  • Personal purchases — market finds, boutique items, souvenirs
  • Additional spa treatments or add-on activities
  • Travel insurance (required — please arrange before arrival)

A great tip: fly into Paris, spend a night or two, then take the train to Bordeaux or all the way to Sainte-Foy-la-Grande. Worth every minute.
