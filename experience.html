A Deeper Dive

The Clarity Uncorked Experience

Everything that happens before, during, and after your week in Bordeaux.

Step One

Arrival & Reflection

Your journey begins before you board your flight. We kick off with a pre-retreat group call to start building community and your personal strategy for change. You'll arrive in Bordeaux already connected to the women you'll be spending the week with.

Upon arrival, settle into our private maison de maître — a beautifully restored manor house surrounded by the rolling vineyards of the Bordeaux region. Peaceful, warm, and designed for introspection and openness.

The Coaching Journey →
Step Two

Immersion & Coaching

The heart of the retreat — curated cultural experiences paired with personalized coaching designed to uncover clarity and new perspectives.

Think market visits and vineyard tours in the morning, long dinners at Le Dix-Sept in the evening, with coaching sessions and facilitated conversations woven throughout. You'll explore Bordeaux, taste the region's finest wines, and find moments of genuine reflection along the way.

Our small group of 6-8 women means real conversations — not a conference, not a crowd.

A Feast for Your Senses →
Step Three

Integration & Next Steps

The week concludes with actionable insights, renewed confidence, and a community of women who've been through something real together.

But it doesn't end when you fly home. We follow up with a post-retreat group integration session and ongoing access to our private community — because the clarity you uncork in Bordeaux deserves to carry forward.
