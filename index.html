Our Story

Born from the best conversations

Clarity Uncorked was born from the idea that the best conversations — the ones that change us — don't happen in conference rooms. They happen around a table, a glass in hand, in those moments when we drop the script and tell the truth about what's really going on.

Picture this: rolling vineyards, strolling local markets, long French dinners, and space to finally exhale. Over days together, we'll uncork old stories, pour out what no longer serves us, and make room for connection, laughter, and clarity.

The Journey

The Clarity Uncorked Experience

Three beautifully designed phases guide you from arrival to transformation and everything in between.

01

Arrival & Reflection

Begin your journey in building community and your strategy for change before you board your flight. Then upon arrival in Bordeaux, settle into peaceful surroundings that invite introspection and openness.

02

Immersion & Coaching

Engage in exciting, curated cultural experiences paired with personalized coaching to uncover clarity and new perspectives across vineyards, markets, and intimate dinners.

What's Included

Everything handled.

This retreat is intentionally designed so you don't have to manage a thing. From the moment you arrive, the week unfolds for you — thoughtfully curated, beautifully paced.

  • Transportation from/to Bordeaux airport or train station
  • Six nights in a private maison de maître
  • Daily breakfast, curated lunches, and multi-course dinners
  • All wine tastings, market visits, and local excursions
  • Pre-retreat group call + 1:1 coaching session
  • On-site coaching throughout the week
  • A small, intentional community of women – not a crowd
  • Post-retreat group integration session and private community access
What's Not Included

A few things to keep in mind

Here's what falls outside the curated retreat experience so you can plan accordingly.

  • Airfare to/from Bordeaux
  • Alcohol or meals outside the curated agenda
  • Personal purchases (market finds, souvenirs, boutique items)
  • Additional spa treatments or add-on activities
  • Travel insurance (required)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the room options?
All rooms are private — only a couple share bathrooms. Each one is unique and thoughtfully decorated, think cozy comfort meets French charm.
Where should I fly into?
Bordeaux is the nearest airport. A lovely option is to fly into Paris, spend a few nights, then take the train to Bordeaux (or even all the way to Sainte-Foy-la-Grande!).
Will there be downtime?
Absolutely. We've intentionally built in space to rest, reflect, journal, explore on your own, or even sneak in a quick work check-in if needed.
What will the weather be like?
Late April is spring in full bloom — expect mild temperatures, sunshine, and maybe a refreshing rain shower. Light layers, comfy shoes, a scarf, and sunglasses are your friends.
Are all meals included?
Yes! All breakfasts, lunches, and dinners are included, along with wine and aperitifs for those who wish to indulge. Delicious non-alcoholic options are always available.
I don't drink alcohol — will I feel excluded?
Not at all. We celebrate presence and pleasure, not pressure. You'll always have sparkling waters, fresh juices, and local specialties — with plenty of company to toast with.
How many people will be in the group?
We're keeping the retreat intentionally small — around 8–10 women — so everyone has space for meaningful connection and personal reflection.
Do I need to speak French?
Not at all. We'll handle logistics and translation. A few mercis and bonjours will go a long way — that's genuinely all you'll need.
Can I come alone?
Absolutely — most guests do! You'll be warmly welcomed into a community of curious, open-hearted women from the very first group call.
I have food allergies — will those be accommodated?
Yes! Please let us know about any allergies, intolerances, or dietary preferences when you register. Our meals are designed with care and everyone is included.
