Our Story

Born from the best conversations

Clarity Uncorked was born from the idea that the best conversations — the ones that change us — don't happen in conference rooms. They happen around a table, a glass in hand, in those moments when we drop the script.

Rolling vineyards, strolling local markets, long French dinners, and space to finally exhale. We'll uncork old stories, pour out what no longer serves us, and make room for connection, laughter, and clarity.

The Journey

The Clarity Uncorked Experience

Three beautifully designed phases guide you from arrival to transformation.

01

Arrival & Reflection

Begin building community and your strategy for change before you board your flight. Upon arrival in Bordeaux, settle into peaceful surroundings that invite introspection.

02

Immersion & Coaching

Curated cultural experiences paired with personalized coaching to uncover clarity and new perspectives — across vineyards, markets, and intimate dinners.

03

Integration & Next Steps

Conclude with actionable insights, renewed confidence, and a supportive community ready to embrace your life's next exciting chapter.

What's Included

Everything handled.

Intentionally designed so you don't have to manage a thing.

  • Transportation from/to Bordeaux airport or train station
  • Six nights in a private maison de maître
  • Daily breakfast, curated lunches, and multi-course dinners
  • All wine tastings, market visits, and local excursions
  • Pre-retreat group call + 1:1 coaching session
  • On-site coaching throughout the week
  • Small, intentional group of women — not a crowd
  • Post-retreat integration session and private community access
What's Not Included

A few things to plan for

Here's what falls outside the retreat so you can budget accordingly.

  • Airfare to/from Bordeaux
  • Alcohol or meals outside the curated agenda
  • Personal purchases and souvenirs
  • Additional spa treatments or add-on activities
  • Travel insurance (required)
