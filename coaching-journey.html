More Than a Retreat

A guided transition wrapped in beauty and clarity

Clarity Uncorked may look like a gorgeous week in the French countryside, but what you're also investing in is a high-touch coaching arc with continuity, depth, and community.

Women arrive at thresholds — career changes, identity shifts, empty nests, rising ambition, or a quiet sense that something needs to shift.

They leave with clarity they can feel, direction they trust, and support that lasts.

The coaching begins before we ever set foot in France and continues long after we return. This isn't a one-off escape. It's a guided transition wrapped in beauty, connection, and clarity.

Before You Arrive

Arrive in France already grounded

You shouldn't spend the first two days decompressing. The pre-retreat coaching eases you into the experience so you show up present, open, and ready.

✦

Pre-Retreat Group Session

A warm, guided session where you meet the other women, understand shared themes, and enter the retreat with comfort rather than awkward introductions.

✦

Pre-Retreat 1:1 Coaching Session

A private session with Jen to set intentions, clear mental clutter, and name what you want this retreat to unlock. You arrive aligned — not overwhelmed.

Start Your Journey
While You're in France

Coaching woven into each day

In France, the coaching becomes the quiet rhythm underneath the beauty and exploration. Not lectures — lived experience paired with guided conversation.

✦

Opening Circle

A grounding conversation that anchors your intention and sets the emotional tone for the week.

✦

Daily Reflection Touchpoint

Short, simple prompts that help you stay connected to yourself as the days unfold.

✦

Two Deep-Dive Workshops

Intimate coaching sessions that uncover patterns, desires, and next steps through guided conversations, not lectures.

✦

Optional 1:1 Coaching During the Week

Space for a private session if something rises up that needs more attention or clarity.

After You Return Home

Clarity that follows you home

The real shift happens when you're back home, moving through your actual life. Post-retreat integration gives you the structure and support to carry your clarity forward instead of losing it to old routines.

1

Clarity That Follows You Home

Insights are only powerful if they translate into life. Integration is built into this experience from the start.

2

Group Integration Session

A live session a few weeks after returning home to reconnect, refine next steps, and anchor what shifted in France.

3

Ongoing Community

Each cohort stays connected through a private WhatsApp group — a warm, steady thread of encouragement, small wins, questions, and companionship. Not a noisy group chat. Just steady, ongoing support from women on similar paths.
